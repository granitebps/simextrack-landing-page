SIMEXTRACK

Simple Expense Tracker

SIMEXTRACK is an app that can track your money in the simplest way you can imagine and very customizable.

Features

Track

You can easily track your money by adding expenses, incomes, or transfer between safebox.

History

If you forget where your money is gone, you can check your money history by date or by category.

Customizable

We provide you some default categories. But you can add your category and safebox.
