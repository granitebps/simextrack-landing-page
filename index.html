SIMEXTRACK

Simple Expense Tracker

SIMEXTRACK is an app that can track your money in the simplest way you can imagine and very customizable.

Features

Track

You can easily track your money by adding expenses, incomes, or transfer between safebox. You also can track your money based on category and safebox

History

If you forget where your money is gone, you can check your money history by date, category, and safebox.

Customizable

We provide you some default categories. But you can add your category and safebox. So you can track your money more specific. You can reset your data if you want to start from fresh.

Online/Offline

We provide 2 version of the app. Online and Offline. Offline version if you don't want to send/save your data to us. And online version if you want to save your data and not losing it if you uninstall the app.
